Favorite Albums

by Daniel Heins

Basic Stats

I made 22 playlists. Each one consists of full albums (or EPs) that I like. I recommend listening to them on Spotify, but you can preview them all here. The total length of all playlists is about 4000 tracks, or ~11 days.

Spotify Playlists

I recently made the swap to listening to music on Spotify primarily. I wanted a way to keep track of all the favorite albums that I've ever listened to, and even though Spotify isn't a great tool for managing an album "collection", this is what I came up with.

Organization

Each playlist has some sort of unique theme, or maybe a unifying genre or two. Maybe you'll find some new music that speaks to you!
